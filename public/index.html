Patient Management Platform for Physical Therapy

A mobile application that will imPacT patient’s adherence to their home exercise programs for the benefit of doctors, clinics, sports teams, and patients.

Features

Eliminate Paper Handouts

Giving patients constant access to better quality videos and pictures through a mobile application.

Increase Adherence
Through Reminders

Allows patients to customize reminders to fit their daily schedule for each exercise. Patients are given exercises that require a different number of sets per day, which is why the customization feature is imperative for increased adherence.

Track Progress

A measurement charts that show the degree that the patient reached for each exercise tested. This allows patients to set goals and increases doctor’s ability to clear patients because the patient will always have their numbers with them at checkups.



How It Works



Step 1

Physical Therapist or Athletic Trainer enters in the HEP that the patient should complete.

Step 2

Patient completes HEP with the help of reminders, easy access to exercises, and progress updates.

Step 3

Physical Therapist or Athletic Trainer updates HEP and progress after each appointment.
